Our Services

Digital QR Restaurant Menu

We provide smart and contactless Digital QR Menus for restaurants and cafés. With just a scan, customers can instantly explore the menu on their smartphones, making dining faster, safer, and more engaging. It helps restaurants reduce printing costs and offer a modern dining experience.

Test Application for Coaching & Institutions

Our powerful Test Application empowers coaching centers, schools, and tutors to create and conduct online or offline tests seamlessly. Faculty members can design customized question papers, evaluate results instantly, and track student performance—all within a user-friendly platform..

Online Sales of Jewelry & Kitchen Essentials

We bring everyday essentials closer to you with our curated range of jewelry and kitchen products available on trusted e-commerce platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and Meesho. Our goal is to provide quality products at affordable prices while reaching customers across India.
